Community Rules

  1. Respect Others: Treat fellow members with courtesy and respect. Personal attacks, harassment, hate speech, and discriminatory remarks are not tolerated.
  2. Stay On Topic: Keep discussions relevant to the forum's theme or specific topic. Off-topic posts may be removed or relocated.
  3. No Spam or Self-Promotion: Avoid posting spam, advertisements, or self-promotional content. This includes unsolicited links or repeated posting of similar content.
  4. Be Constructive: Contribute meaningfully to discussions. Critique ideas, not individuals. Provide evidence and reasoning to support your viewpoints.
  5. Use Appropriate Language: Refrain from using offensive, vulgar, or inappropriate language. Keep discussions professional and suitable for all ages.
  6. Respect Privacy: Do not share personal information of yourself or others without permission. Respect the confidentiality of private communications.
  7. No Plagiarism: Provide proper attribution when sharing content or ideas that are not your own. Respect copyright laws and intellectual property rights.
  8. Moderation Decisions: Accept and abide by decisions made by moderators. Contact them privately if you have concerns about moderation.
  9. Help Maintain the Community: Report any violations of these guidelines to moderators promptly. Encourage a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere.
  10. Stay Safe: Be cautious about sharing sensitive information online. Report any suspicious or malicious activity to moderators or administrators.
